Devansh's movie recommendations

My favourite three movies

1. Interstellar

Thought provoking and intriguing, keeping you on the edge of the seat constantly.

2. 3 Idiots

Makes you laugh your ass off for 150 minutes while simulatneously teaching you important life lessons on the importance of individuality, passion and the outdated education system of our country

3. Shawshank Redemption

Teaches us the importance of hope with excellent writing and great pacing
